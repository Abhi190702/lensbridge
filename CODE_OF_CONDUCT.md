# Code of Conduct

LensBridge follows the spirit of the Contributor Covenant.

## Our Standard

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Assume good intent, but accept correction quickly. Technical disagreement is welcome; personal attacks are not.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information.
- Repeated low-effort disruption.
- Security disclosure misuse.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
