# Design System

The canonical design notes live in [../DESIGN.md](../DESIGN.md).

## Desktop

- Dark graphite base.
- One accent color.
- Clear two-zone dashboard: connection and live output.
- Primary action near stream: **Open OBS Output**.
- Planned pages stay quiet and clearly labeled.

## Phone

- Camera first.
- Large thumb-friendly stream action.
- Plain local privacy note.
- Advanced quality controls should stay secondary.

## OBS Output

The capture surface is intentionally plain:

- Full-window video.
- Solid background.
- No sidebar.
- No topbar.
- No statusbar.
- No QR code.
- No cards.
- Auto-hiding controls.
